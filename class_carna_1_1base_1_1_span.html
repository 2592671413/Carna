template<typename T>
bool Carna::base::Span< T >::operator< ( const Span< T >  another) const [inline]

Compares this span to another.

The ordering is determined by the spans' first attribute. If they are equal in first, the ordering is defined based on the comparison of their last attribute. The spans' natural order is implied by the natural order of T.
