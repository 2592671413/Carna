Detailed Description

Renderer component that defines the point of view and perspective of the spectator.

Speaking strictly, this' class main purpose is to define coordinate mapping from scene space into screen space, which it does by supplying the projection- and the camera matrices:

Implementations are also capable of performing any custom GL operations before and after the usual drawing: refer to openShutter and closeShutter.

See also:
DefaultCamera is a commonly used implementation.
