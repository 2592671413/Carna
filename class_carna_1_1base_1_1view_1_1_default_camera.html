Also see the documentation on the produced camera matrices and projection matrices.

Obtains the camera matrix.

See also:
Refer to Coordinate Systems for more information on vertex transformation.

The produced matrix spans the camera space coordinate system, so that the scene space is mapped - only using proportional scaling - and the whole volume data becomes subset of

\[ \big\{ \vec p = ( x, y, z )^\mathrm T : x, y, z \in [0, 1] \big\} \]

Hence, the length of one unit in camera space is chosen equal to the length of the volume data's longest edge. The scene is translated two units into the deep.
