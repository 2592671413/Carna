Detailed Description

Additional VolumeRenderings sub-module, that implements the CT volume visualization as a multi-channel maximum intensity projection.

Provides classes related to the volume renderer maximum intensity projection mode.

You might be looking for the VolumeRendererMode MIP implementation.
